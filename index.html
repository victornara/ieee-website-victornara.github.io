Welcome To The IEEE

Our primary mission is to provide a usable lab space for the purpose of completing coursework or personal projects to all students of Michigan Technological University, as well as the general community. We keep a fully stocked electronics lab with tools, software, and free common parts to be used by our members. To see what we are capable of, check the Lab Description.
